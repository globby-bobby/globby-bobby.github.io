First turn player moves
You may move in any of the four directions (left, right, up, down)
Second turn player can move or shoot
If shooting, hit ENTER
Press arrow keys to aim projectile
Press ENTER to lock in aiming
Use left and right arrows to select how much ammo you will use
Every ammo adds +1 radius to the bomb's explosion
When out of ammo, you must wait 2 turns (2 moves) to regen one ammo
After moving 15 times, your third ammo slot is replaced by the NUKE
The nuke's explosion has a radius of 4 tiles and destroys any nearby destructable tiles
